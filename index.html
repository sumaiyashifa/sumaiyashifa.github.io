About Me

Who I Am

I'm a final-year Computer Science & Engineering undergraduate at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), expected to graduate in 2026. My academic journey has been driven by a deep curiosity about how machines understand and generate language.

My thesis explores Mixture of Experts (MoE) architectures for Grammatical Error Correction (GEC) in Bangla, integrating optimization techniques to improve model efficiency on low-resource languages. I also actively contribute to research in NLP, materials science, and bioinformatics through collaborations.

Beyond research, I build full-stack applications — from a Laravel-based job portal to a deep-learning powered plant disease detector with a React Native frontend. I believe research and engineering are most powerful when they work together.

I received the Dean's Award in my 3rd year for academic excellence, and I'm currently exploring bioinformatics, particularly network-based analysis for autoimmune disease target discovery.
